Why CXOEra?

The CXOEra event is designed by a group of passionate women founders who led the Advanced analytic divisions from the fortune 500 firms. Founded in 2016. CXOEra leverages unique access to the world’s most influential IT leaders, research analysts and venture capitalists - uniting these global forces to harness their collective knowledge, address today’s critical issues and discover innovative solutions.

We wish to help organizations get a different perspective of the challenges they are facing, with the ultimate goal of enhancing their business. We recognize that sharing our experiences enriches all of us.
